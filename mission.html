Our mission is simple:
To connect young people with fresh date ideas, and provide businesses with data about those people.
And solves two pain points with that mission.

Young people (18 - 25) want creative date options quickly and easily.

"Online calendars are almost never well curated. I often have to search through multiple ones and sometimes don't find anything."
- Female, (18 - 25)
"I want to find something to do on a date other than just going out to eat."
- Male, (18 - 25)
"I tend to look at online listings but find it difficult to know what to be looking for or how to put things together."
- Male, (18 - 25)

Businesses lack data about their customers, but identify that as a growing need.

"We don't do data collection. I think we see value in knowing about our users."
- Casey Prince, Executive Director of Theatre Cedar Rapids
"We don't take the time to deal with and collect the information. We don't spend a lot of time on what we probably should to interact with and retain customers."
- Kory Nanke, Owner of Reds, NewBo Alehouse, Red Frog and more
"I see value, it helps us in marketing who to target... It would allow us to validate our customer's information."
- Rona Wyant, Owner Fireside Winery
So we know businesses want data, and people want new and exciting events. But how do we make money?
Step one: Aquiring users, and gathering data

27 people have already signed up via our website to become early beta testers.

Step two: Connect those people to local businesses and fun dates.
Step three: Monetize

Phase 1

Charge for display advertising within the application to promote local businesses.

Phase 2

Charge businesses to access the user data so they can better target their deals and maximize customer returns.

Phase 3

Charge a premium subscription to users who want access to exclusive deals.
We talked to the owners or managers of 10 area businesses who stated that they would discuss paying for access to user data.
