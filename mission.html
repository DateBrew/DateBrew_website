Our Mission is Simple:
To connect customers with exciting, new businesses, and provide those businesses with data about their customers.
And when asked if they would pay for that data, they said:
We don't do data collection. I think we see value in knowing about our users.
- Casey Prince, Executive Director of Theatre Cedar Rapids
The data would show us things that we are missing, being here is only half of it... Having a reliable source of information like that is always beneficial.

- Thomas Kacena, Manager of Cedar Rapids Bowling Center.
I see value, it helps us in marketing who to target... It would allow us to validate our customer's information.
- Rona Wyant, Owner Fireside Winery
